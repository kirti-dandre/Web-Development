Jyotirao Phule & Savitribai Phule

Great Social Reformers of India

Introduction

Jyotirao Phule and Savitribai Phule were pioneers of social reform who fought against caste discrimination and promoted education for all.

Jyotirao Phule

  • Born: 1827
  • Founded Satyashodhak Samaj
  • Worked against caste system
  • Wrote "Gulamgiri"

Savitribai Phule

  • Born: 1831
  • First Female Teacher of India
  • Started first girls school
  • Worked for women rights

Contributions

  • Started girls education in India
  • Fought against child marriage
  • Worked for equality

Inspiration

"Education is the most powerful weapon to change society."
